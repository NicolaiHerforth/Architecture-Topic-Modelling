Anders Bundgaard (1864-1937): “Og dagen rinder med sine tusinde krav” (1929-35). Skovbogårds Alle/Valby Langgade. A naked female, scared of the thousand daily demands  presses herself against a protecting male. Copenhagen wit named it “bare bum” ("Bar numse"). The marble is from Carrara, same site where Michelangelo got it.
