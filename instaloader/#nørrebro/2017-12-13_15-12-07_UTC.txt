“What on Earth is a Christmas cone??”, I’m often asked.
.
It’s a traditional Christmas ornament (#kræmmerhus), usually homemade of glitzy paper and hung on the Christmas tree with sweets in. This is my porcelain version. I hang them on a string across the window, sometimes with flowers in.
