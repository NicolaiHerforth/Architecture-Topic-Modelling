Tracked down another craft beer nirvana in Copenhagen ... Warpigs. Set in the old meat processing plant to the west of the city centre (Vesterbro). 22 beers on tap - all brewed by Warpigs - a collaboration between 3 Floyds from the US and Denmark’s Mikkeller. We tried a ‘L’il drunk baby’ session IPA (left in photo) - at 6.6% it would be a short session on the full on tropical assault up front followed by a long and balanced bitter and fruity sweet finish. And a ‘young lightning’ APA (right in photo) - the tropical and bitter flavours are reigned in with a hint of sightly chewey malt. #warpigs #vesterbro #copenhagen
