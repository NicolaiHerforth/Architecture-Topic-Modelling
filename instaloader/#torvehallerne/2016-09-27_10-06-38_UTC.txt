Hello, a super sweet breakfast in the Torvehallerne market. Actually, we bought this pastry in the nearby bakery - "Naturbageriet". All these buns together costed us ~ 100 DKK, while only 1 bun at the market costed 75 DKK :) They also have vegan or gluten-free options available, which is really nice if you want to try some Danish pastry in those variations :)
P.S. After this trip I was actually craving fresh fruits and vegetables sooo badly.
