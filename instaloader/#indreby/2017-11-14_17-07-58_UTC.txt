Lunchin’ a la Italiana in Copenhagen is what I wanna do everyday, all day 😍🇩🇰🇮🇹
.
What a pleasant surprise was to spontaneously visit Spaghetteria La Perla, the first actual Spaghetteria in town.
This time I tried the lunch menu, in which most of their dishes -including pastas and pizzas- are incredibly priced at 79 DKK.
We tried pesto and chicken pasta and a creamy bacon, pea and tomato pasta.
Both substantial, delicious and perfectly cooked 👏🏽
Throw in a couple of Aperol spritzes and life doesn’t get much better than that.
Bon appetit a tutti ❤️🇮🇹!
