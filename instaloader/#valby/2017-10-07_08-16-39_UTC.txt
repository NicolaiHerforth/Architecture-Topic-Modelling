Today is Limu coffee beans day at our roastery. 
Limu is a high-quality washed Ethiopian coffee bean grown at elevations ranging from 1,800 to 2,100 meters in the southwest region. It has a relatively low acidity yet is slightly sharp. 
When the coffee is made, you can easily distinguish its well-balanced body with a spicy flavor with a pleasant sweet and vibrant floral overtone.
