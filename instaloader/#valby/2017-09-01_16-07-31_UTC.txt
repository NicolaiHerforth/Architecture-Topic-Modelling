J. C. Jacobsen (1811-1887) Founder of world famous Carlsberg Breweries, named after his son, Carl and the hill (bjerg or berg) near the then small village Valby. Like the editors of Politivennen, Jacobsen was captain of the Copenhagen civil militsia, Borgervæbningen, and minded for the bourgeois society.
