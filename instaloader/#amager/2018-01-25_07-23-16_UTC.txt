An extension to a community center, “Kvarterhuset” by Dorte Mandrup Architects from 2001. This glass section of a much larger refurbishment of an older building is an assembly hall for parties and gatherings balancing on crooked wooden pillars. Behind the glass facade you see a transparent plywood frame.
