Smag - Copenhagen, Denmark 
The best sandwich I have EVER had! There I said it! And I don't mind that I'll never be able to take it back ☺️
Sweet potato purée, chicken, peppers, red pesto and salad on dark rye seeded bread. Yum. 10/10!
Get your fix at the Torvehallerne food market near Nørreport Station.
