📍Copenhagen, Denmark, Hija de Sanchez.

#Mexican food in #Denmark? Wouldn't be my first choice but my boy was craving some, so we accommodated him. We got a #taco combo platter with #cheese, #lengua (beef tongue), and #carnitas(pork belly), and 2 more orders of lengua. It's not bad but I had better in California.

在#丹麥 吃#墨西哥菜 不會是我的首選，但是兒子最近迷上墨西哥菜我們就配合他吃一下。這家在#哥本哈根 小有名氣，味道不錯但是還是在加州的墨西哥菜好吃。我們點了一份綜合盤（#起司，#牛舌，#豬五花），又加兩國牛舌。
