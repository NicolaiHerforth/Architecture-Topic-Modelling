A Dance on the Window of a Bus in the Rain
*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~
The seat beneath me vibrates and hums with the engine.  The strange chalky fabric scratches at jeans.  I hug my bag in close to me.  Nerves.  Perhaps.  A slight gaze to the right, at the window, but I don’t look out.  A droplet of water trickles down the glass.  It’s raining. ~*~*~*~
