Delicous Danish Smorrebrod! (Open sandwich)

Danish meatballs with potato salad, red onion, chive, tomato decorarated beautifully on buttered rye bread.

Visually appealing as well as satisfyingly tasty. 🍽🍽🍽 I wish I have more opportunities to try other combinations too. Salmon, Steak, shrimp, roast pork, roast beef, and many others.
