Fantastic that so many people are raving and going completely nuts over our vegan SURINAAMSE DAME peanut soup (pun intended) 🥜😅 Since the very beginning, it was an instant classic. It's a "living soup", meaning that we keep on adding / changing ingredients and adjusting the cooking process👩🏽‍🍳✨ It has around 20 components.
